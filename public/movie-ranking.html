The Best Binge-watch-worthy series/movies According to Divya

My top 3 of all-time.

YOU

I like You because it's a perfect mix of psychological thriller, mystery, and dark romance. The way the story is told from Joe Goldberg's perspective makes it even more intriguing, giving a deep dive into his obsessive yet calculated mind. The series keeps me hooked with its unpredictable twists, intense storytelling, and morally complex characters. Every season adds new layers to the story, making it impossible to stop watching. It's thrilling, creepy, and oddly addictive!

OK JAANU

I like OK Jaanu because it's a lighthearted and feel-good romantic movie with great chemistry between Aditya Roy Kapur and Shraddha Kapoor. The story explores modern relationships, dreams, and the struggles of balancing love with career ambitions. The music by A.R. Rahman adds so much charm, especially songs like Enna Sona and The Humma Song. It's a sweet, refreshing watch that perfectly captures the essence of young love and the excitement of new beginnings!

MONEY HEIST

Money Heist is an intense, brilliantly written heist drama with mind-blowing twists and strategic storytelling. The Professor's mastermind planning, the emotional depth of the characters, and the high-stakes action make it super addictive. Every episode keeps me on edge with unexpected turns and powerful moments.
